Authors: Mary Hale, Josue Villalobos, Colette Apostol

Project Summary: We explored the relationship between a country’s human rights index score in relation to the country’s average number of years in formal schooling for a 25 year old adult. A human rights index score is how much a country practices and respects human rights, ranging from 0 to 1 (with 1 being very respectful), and the data came from Our World In Data.
